How to Play:

There are 5 chapters, each with 3 lanes, and you must click in each lane (left, middle, right) to avoid the KMT baddies.

This game does not keep score. The only way to win, is to survive until you reach Luoyang.

(There's always a button, in the left top corner, that will let you progress as quickly as you prefer!)

Begin playing!
